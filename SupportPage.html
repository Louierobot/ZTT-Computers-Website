Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the warranty period for your PCs?
All our custom-built PCs come with a standard 2-year warranty. Gaming PCs include an extended 3-year warranty on major components.
Do you offer on-site support in Southampton?
Yes, we provide on-site technical support within Southampton and surrounding areas. Service charges may apply based on location and type of support needed.
What's included in your build service?
Our build service includes component selection, professional assembly, extensive testing, Windows installation, and basic software setup. We also provide a detailed build report.
How do I claim warranty?
To claim warranty, simply fill out our support form with your order number and issue description. Our team will respond within 24 hours with next steps.

Our Warranty Policy

We stand behind the quality of our builds with comprehensive warranty coverage.

  • 2-year standard warranty on all custom builds
  • 3-year extended warranty on gaming PCs
  • 1-year warranty on refurbished systems
  • Free labor for warranty repairs
  • Parts replacement with equivalent or better components
  • Local pickup and delivery for warranty services
